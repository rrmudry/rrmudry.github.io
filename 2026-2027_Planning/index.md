## Lab & Activity Ideas
- [ ] "Why Units Matter" Activity: Use the unit-less examples as lesson starters:
    - "The house is 3 high" (Feet? Meters? Stories?)
    - "Add 5 sugar to the recipe" (Grains? Grams? Cups?)
    - "It's 20 out" (K? °F? °C?)

## Common Student Pitfalls (Scaffolding Ideas)
- [ ] Confusing units with variables (e.g., using 'm' for meters vs. 'm' for mass).
- [ ] Confusing "How far" with "How long": Students often answer distance questions with time units ("five minutes away") and vice versa.
