# Not accepting Sub-standard work

One of the real problems that we had this year was students thinking that it is ok to turn in essentially elementary or lower grade quality work.  There is a difference between finishing something and turning in something that is complete and on grade level.

Next school year, I want to build in ways for the students to collect data then transition it into professional level data presentations or projects.
