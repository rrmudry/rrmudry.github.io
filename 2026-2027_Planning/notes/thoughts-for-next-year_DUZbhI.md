# Thoughts for next year

Some of the things I want to improve for next year:

1. Teach the students to be self reliant.
  a. One of the big things that I want to teach is following directions.
  b. Another thing that I want to teach is continuing to try even when they are stuck.
2. Do not allow learned helplessness.
3. Have a positive atmosphere, but do not allow students to sit wherever they want.
  a. This will mean enforcing seating chart
  b. This will mean not being too flexible when students make requests to move seats or sit with friends.
  c. I would like to change groups throughout the school year.
