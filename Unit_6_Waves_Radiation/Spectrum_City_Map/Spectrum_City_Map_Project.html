📅 Daily Pacing & Accountability

Do not move on to the next phase until the current one is complete. Get your sub's sign-off for each phase.

  • Phase 1: The Map Layout Collect one 11x17" landscape sheet. Lightly sketch the 7 zones on your paper. Draw the "Wave Baseline" at the bottom.
  • Phase 2: Architecture & Visuals Draw the required buildings and features for all 7 zones. Begin adding color (colored pencils/markers).
  • Phase 3: The Physics Labels (Final Polish) Add all required science labels, hazard warnings, and interaction arrows (such as the "Lowest Frequency" science label, the "Hazard Level: EXTREME" warning, and reflection/absorption arrows). Complete rubric.
  • Overall City Design The map is fully colored with no large, empty white spaces.

📊 Grading Rubric (How to get your A)

The Wave Baseline

Excellent: Continuous wave perfectly shows decreasing wavelength/increasing frequency left to right.

Needs Work: Wave is drawn, but doesn't clearly change frequency.

Visual Requirements

Excellent: All 7 zones include specific required drawings. Map is colored and vibrant.

Needs Work: Missing required drawings or coloring is messy/incomplete.

Science Labels

Excellent: Every zone has exact Science, Hazard, and Physics labels requested.

Needs Work: Labels present but missing key vocabulary (Ionizing, Frequency, etc.).

Physics Interactions

Excellent: Arrows clearly/correctly show Reflection and Absorption in Zones 4 & 6.

Needs Work: Arrows drawn but don't clearly show the physics concept.
