📋 Technical Operations

PLAYING THE GAME (FRONT VIEW):

1. Drag the **Red Probe tip** to any Question peg.

2. Drag the **Black Probe tip** to any Answer peg.

3. If they match the internal electrical wiring connected on the back of the box, the circuit closes and the **light bulb glows!** 💡

WIRING THE BOX (BACK VIEW):

1. Click a peg on the left, then drag your wire to any peg on the right to establish a secret pathway.

2. Double-click any peg to clear its wire connection.

3. Toggle back to **PLAY FRONT** to test your custom circuitry wiring!
